Venue Information

The Daily Show studio is located on 11th Ave. between 51st and 52nd St. in Manhattan.

For discounted parking, audience members can park at the K Group parking lot on the corner of 51st street and the West Side Highway. Audience members who get their parking ticket validated after the taping by our staff will be able to park for $14 for regular sized vehicles and $24 for oversized vehicles.
